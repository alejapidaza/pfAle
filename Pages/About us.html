OUR BRAND

Our brand is the result of years of work and learning that resulted in a great passion and love for clothes. The clothes we wear are our identity and should reflect everything we are. That is why we created a comfortable, light and versatile brand.

VALUE PROPOSAL

We believe in handmade work, quality and the importance of small details. From design to tailoring, we seek to build a sustainable brand on two main fronts: to fairly remunerate our labour and to reduce the use of polluting materials.
